# Engineering Decision #004

## Title

Standard Lesson Format

---

## Status

Accepted

---

## Context

Every lesson should feel familiar and follow the same learning process.

---

## Decision

Every lesson follows the same structure:

- Learn the concept
- Build examples
- Complete three mini projects
- Update documentation
- Update the Knowledge Base

---

## Why

Consistency reduces cognitive load and allows learning to focus on programming rather than structure.

---

## Alternatives Considered

- Different format for every lesson.

---

## Consequences

### Positive

✔ Predictable workflow

✔ Easier navigation

✔ Better long-term learning

### Negative

• Less flexibility for unusual lessons.
