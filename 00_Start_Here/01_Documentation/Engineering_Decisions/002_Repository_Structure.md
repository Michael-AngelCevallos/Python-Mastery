# Engineering Decision #002

## Title

Repository Structure

---

## Status

Accepted

---

## Context

Before beginning Python Mastery, a long-term folder structure was needed that could grow from beginner lessons to advanced projects without becoming disorganized.

---

## Decision

The repository is organized into numbered learning phases, supporting resources, portfolio projects, documentation, and capstone work.

Major folders include:

- 00_Start_Here
- 01_Fundamentals
- 02_Intermediate
- 03_Object_Oriented_Programming
- ...
- Portfolio_Projects

---

## Why

A consistent structure makes navigation predictable and allows the repository to grow without major reorganization.

---

## Alternatives Considered

- Store everything in one folder.
- Organize only by project.
- Organize only by topic.

---

## Consequences

### Positive

✔ Easy navigation

✔ Scales well

✔ Future website generation

### Negative

• Requires planning before development begins.
