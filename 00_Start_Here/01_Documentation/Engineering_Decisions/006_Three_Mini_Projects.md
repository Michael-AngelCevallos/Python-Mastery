# Engineering Decision #006

## Title

Three Mini Projects Per Lesson

---

## Status

Accepted

---

## Context

Simply reading about a topic is not enough to master it.

---

## Decision

Every lesson ends with three progressively more challenging mini projects.

---

## Why

Repetition through building creates long-term understanding.

---

## Alternatives Considered

- One large project
- Unlimited practice
- No projects

---

## Consequences

### Positive

✔ Reinforces learning

✔ Builds confidence

✔ Creates portfolio material

### Negative

• Slightly increases lesson time.
