# Engineering Principle #007

## Principle

Solve problems with the smallest safe change possible.

---

## Meaning

Avoid changing more than necessary.

Small changes reduce risk.

---

## Why It Matters

Limiting the scope of changes makes debugging easier and keeps systems more secure.

---

## Example

Instead of changing the execution policy for the entire computer, I changed it only for my Windows user account.

---

## Key Takeaway

Small changes solve problems while minimizing unintended consequences.
